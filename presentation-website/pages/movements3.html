Czech New Wave

A few important Czech New Wave directors;

  • Jiří Menzel his film Ostre sledované vlaky

    Jiří Menzel was a Czech film director, theatre director, actor, and screenwriter. His films often combine a humanistic view of the world with sarcasm and provocative cinematography

  • Jan Nemec and his film Démanty noci

    Jan Nemec, whose surreal, parable-like films made him one of the leading lights of the Czech new wave in the 1960s, died on March 18 in Prague.

  • Miloš Forman and his film Horí, má panenko

    Miloš Forman, Czech-born New Wave filmmaker who was known primarily for the distinctively American movies that he made after his immigration to the United States. Forman was an important figure in the Czechoslovak New Wave.
